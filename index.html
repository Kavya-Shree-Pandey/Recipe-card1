Delicious Pasta

Indulgent pasta creations,bringing Italy to your plate.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp oliv oil
  • 2-3 cloves garlic(chopped)
  • 1 medium onion(chopped)
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black papper
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flacks

Instructions

  1. Mix flour,salt,little water and knead smooth and rest 20 mins.
  2. roll dough thin and cut into pasta shapes,dust with flour.
  3. In salted water,cook fresh pata 2-4mins and drain.
  4. Saute onion and garlic,add tomatoes with herbs & spices.
  5. Toss pasta in sauce,add cheese & basil, serve hot.
Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings:2 Total Time: 30 mins
